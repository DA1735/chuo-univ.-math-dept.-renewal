\begin{itemize}

 \item[\bul] {\bf Aim}

The objective of the $B\Gamma$ School is
first of all to review the principal results
in the algebraic topology of foliations
and secondly to search for the future developments.
Some introductory lectures are also planned.

 \item[\bul] {\bf Date and Venue}

September 29th (Mon.) -- October 2nd (Thu.), 2003
\\

\item[\bul] \hypertarget{TA}{{\bf Titles}}
\vspace{3mm}

{\bf Hurder, Steven (University of Illinois at Chicago):}\vspace{1mm}

\hspace{5mm}I.\ Introduction to $B\Gamma$\\
\hspace{5mm}II.\ Rational homotopy theory of $B\Gamma$\\
\hspace{5mm}III.\ $B\Gamma$ tool box
\vspace{2mm}

{\bf Mitsumatsu, Yoshihiko (Chuo University):}\vspace{1mm}

\hspace{5mm}I.\ Bott vanishing, residues, and secondary classes\\
\hspace{5mm}II.\ Thurston \& Heitsch's independent variation, etc.
\vspace{2mm}

{\bf Morita, Shigeyuki (University of Tokyo):}\vspace{1mm}

\hspace{5mm}I.\ Discontinuous invariants of foliations I\\
\hspace{5mm}II.\ Discontinuous invariants of foliations II
\vspace{2mm}

{\bf Schweitzer S.J., Paul A.\ (PUC-Rio):}\vspace{1mm}

\hspace{5mm}I.\ Introduction to Gel'fand-Fuks cohomology\\
\hspace{5mm}II.\ The injectivity conjecture
\vspace{2mm}

{\bf Sergiescu, Vlad (Universit\'{e} de Grenoble):}\vspace{1mm}

\hspace{5mm}I.\ P.\ Greenberg's work on PL foliations\\
\hspace{5mm}II.\ Informal seminar
\vspace{2mm}

{\bf Tsuboi, Takashi (University of Tokyo):}\vspace{1mm}

\hspace{5mm}I.\ Introduction to the Mather-Thurston theory\\
\hspace{5mm}II.\ Connectivity in low differentiability
\vspace{2mm}

{\bf Vogt, Elmar (Freie Universit\"{a}t Berlin):}\vspace{1mm}

\hspace{5mm}I.\ Segal's and Moerdijk's construction of $B\Gamma$\\
\hspace{5mm}II.\ Differentiable cohomology for $B\Gamma$\\
\hspace{5mm}III.\ Real homotopy theory
\vspace{3mm}
